    Frequently Asked Questions

    What time is it in New York right now?
    New York follows Eastern Time (ET). During standard time it's UTC-5, and during daylight saving time it's UTC-4. Use our live clock above for the exact current time in New York City.
    What time is it in London now?
    London uses Greenwich Mean Time (GMT/UTC+0) in winter and British Summer Time (BST/UTC+1) in summer. Our world clock shows the precise current time in London.
    How many time zones are there in the world?
    There are 24 standard time zones in the world, but including offsets like UTC+5:30 (India) and UTC+5:45 (Nepal), there are actually 38 distinct time zones currently in use.
    What time is it in Tokyo right now?
    Tokyo, Japan uses Japan Standard Time (JST), which is UTC+9. Japan does not observe daylight saving time, so Tokyo is always 9 hours ahead of UTC year-round.
    How do I convert time between time zones?
    Simply search for any city in our search bar above. We show the current local time instantly. You can compare times between cities by opening multiple city pages.
    Is the weather data updated in real-time?
    Yes! We use the Open-Meteo weather API which provides hourly updated weather data including temperature, humidity, wind speed and weather conditions for cities worldwide.
